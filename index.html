About Us

FoodZone is a vibrant culinary destination with two thriving locations in Southeast Texas, one in Porter and the other in New Caney.

Our journey began in 2018 with the opening of our Porter location, which quickly became a local favorite, featuring 24 diverse food vendors offering everything from savory street eats to sweet treats.

Building on that success, we expanded to New Caney in 2023, introducing a fresh experience with 18 carefully curated vendors surrounding a welcoming green space at the center, perfect for families, friends, and food lovers to gather and relax.

At FoodZone, we are all about community, flavor, and creating a space where great food brings people together.
